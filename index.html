CashCro

Welcome to CashCro, a smart contract token deployed on the Cronos chain. CashCro tokenomics are designed to reward investors with the highest passive return in the market; 10% rewards in CRO on each buy and sell transaction.

With such a high payout the goal of financial freedom can be achieved simply by buying and holding this token. Your rewards can be tracked with our rewards tracker Dapp.
